# Privacy Policy for Mute and Trim

This privacy policy applies to the **Mute and Trim** mobile application (the "Application") operated by Slava (the "Service Provider").

## Data Collection

**The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Mute and Trim is a fully offline video processing tool. It operates entirely on your device:

- Videos you select are processed locally using Android's built-in `MediaExtractor` and `MediaMuxer` APIs
- The Application does **not** connect to the internet
- The Application does **not** include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
- No user data is sent to any server, API, or third party
- No cookies, beacons, or tracking technologies are used

## File Access

The Application uses Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF) to let you pick video files. The picked file is read temporarily for processing, and the resulting muted or trimmed video is saved to your device's Downloads folder. The Application does not access any files outside of what you explicitly select.

## Third Parties

The Application does not share any data with third parties because no data leaves your device.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the Application's functionality changes. Continued use after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
